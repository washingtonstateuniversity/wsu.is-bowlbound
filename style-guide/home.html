Monday, December 26

Bank of America Kickoff Luncheon

  • Time: 11:00 A.M.
  • Location: Hilton San Diego Bayfront, Sapphire Ballroom 1 Park Blvd., San Diego

Over 1,00 guests annually attend this can’t-miss bowl event and are treated to a great lunch, the heard-pounding sounds of the two university bands, video team highlight presentations and team interviews. Guests have the opportunity to sit and interact with the players and coaches of the participating schools.

The event begins at 11:00 am with a “Battle of the Bands.” At the end of the band concert, guests will make their way to the ballroom where they will have the change to sit and eat with players from their favorite school. In addition to a fantastic lunch, they will be treated to a program featuring the head coaches, team captains and ESPN personalities, as well as highlight reals from each team.
